CLXX

The stars that wander through the desert night,
Are but the shadows of thy brighter fire;
For thou dost shine with a more holy light,
The only object of my soul's desire.
The moon may change her fashion every day,
And hide her face within a silver cloud,
But thy perfection never fades away,
Nor is thy glory wrapped within a shroud.
Then let me be the planet of thy sun,
And move within the circle of thy grace,
Until the race of all my days is run,
And I behold thee in a better place.
For in thy love I find my only rest,
The happy island of the ever-blessed.
